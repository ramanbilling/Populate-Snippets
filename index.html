Information We Do NOT Collect

Populate Snippets is designed with privacy as the foundation. Here's what we explicitly do not collect:

  • Your snippets or their content - We never see what you type in your snippets
  • Personal information - No names, emails, addresses, or identifying data
  • Browsing history - We don't track which websites you visit
  • Usage analytics - We don't know how often you use snippets or which ones
  • Typing data - We don't log your keystrokes or what you type
  • Device information - No fingerprinting or device tracking

Permissions Explained

Populate Snippets requires certain Chrome permissions to function. Here's exactly why we need each one:

  • Storage - To save your snippets locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API
  • Active Tab - To detect when you type the trigger phrase (e.g., "pop.") and show snippet suggestions
  • Clipboard Write - To copy expanded snippets to your clipboard for pasting

How Your Data is Stored

When you create snippets, they are stored using Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage.local). This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is encrypted by Chrome's storage system
  • Is only accessible by the Populate Snippets extension
  • Is never transmitted over the internet
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the extension

Data Export & Backup

You can export all your snippets at any time through the extension's settings. The export file is created locally on your device. We recommend backing up your snippets regularly if you have many important ones.
